The Royalwood Neighbourhood Association Board is providing advance notice of proposed amendments to the association's bylaws. As required by our governance framework, all members will have the opportunity to review and vote on these changes at the 2025 Annual General Meeting.

## Proposed Changes

### Section 4 — Membership

The current wording ties voting membership to property ownership. The proposed amendment expands voting eligibility to **all adult residents of Royalwood**, including renters, provided they have attended at least one RNA general meeting in the preceding 12 months.

**Rationale:** A growing portion of our neighbourhood includes renters, particularly in the newer multi-family units on the east side. The board believes that all community members who have a stake in neighbourhood decisions should have a voice.

### Section 7 — Board Quorum

The current quorum requirement for board decisions is five of seven board members. The proposal reduces this to **four of seven**, to better accommodate scheduling difficulties that have occasionally delayed time-sensitive decisions.

**Rationale:** In the past two years, two board decisions were delayed by more than three weeks due to quorum requirements during holiday periods. This change preserves democratic oversight while allowing the board to function more responsively.

## How to Provide Feedback

The board encourages all residents to review the [full draft bylaws](/resources/bylaws-overview) available in the Resources section. Written feedback can be submitted to [board@royalwoodna.org](mailto:board@royalwoodna.org) prior to April 30, 2025.

All feedback will be summarized and shared with attendees at the AGM. The vote will take place at the end of the meeting.
