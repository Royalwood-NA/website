What a morning! Despite a bit of a chill in the air (it *is* still March in the prairies), more than 40 residents of all ages turned out for our annual Spring Community Cleanup on March 22nd.

## By the Numbers

- **40+** volunteers
- **18** bags of litter collected
- **3** sections of pathway restored
- **1** very muddy dog who wandered in uninvited (and was a crowd favourite)

## Where We Worked

Teams spread out across three zones:

1. **Storm pond perimeter** — A perennial target for windblown litter; looking much better now.
2. **Greenway pathways** — Volunteers cleared debris, broken branches, and residual sand from the winter maintenance crews.
3. **Park entrance areas** — Fresh and welcoming for the season ahead.

## Thank You to Our Sponsors

Thank you to the City of Edmonton's NeighbourWorks program for supplying gloves, bags, and pickup tools. And a big thank you to the residents who brought thermoses of hot coffee — you know who you are, and you are heroes.
