After months of planning and volunteer effort, the Royalwood Neighbourhood Association is proud to unveil our completely redesigned website. Whether you're a long-time resident or brand new to the neighbourhood, this site is built with you in mind.

## What's New

Our previous website served us well, but the community deserved something more accessible, easier to navigate, and better suited to how people browse today — whether on a phone, tablet, or desktop.

Here's what you'll find:

- **Blog** — Dated updates from the board and volunteers. Subscribe via RSS or just check back regularly.
- **Resources** — A structured library of governance documents, neighbourhood guides, city info, safety resources, and environmental initiatives.
- **Events** — Upcoming meetings, cleanups, and socials, all in one place.

## How to Get Involved

This website is maintained by RNA volunteers. If you'd like to contribute a post, submit a correction, or help with content, reach out through our [contact page](/contact). We welcome community voices.

## Thank You

A sincere thank you to everyone who provided feedback during the redesign process, and to the volunteers who made this possible. Royalwood is a great place to live because of the people who care about it.

We look forward to keeping you informed and connected.

— The Royalwood NA Board
