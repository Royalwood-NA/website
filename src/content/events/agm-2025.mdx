The 2025 Annual General Meeting of the Royalwood Neighbourhood Association was held on November 13, 2025. Here's a recap of what was discussed and decided at this important annual gathering.

## Meeting Overview

This year's AGM brought together over 80 residents to review the RNA's activities from 2025, approve the 2026 budget, and elect the board for the coming year.

## Key Decisions & Approvals

### 2025 Accomplishments

The board highlighted several significant achievements from 2025:

- **Spring Cleanup** — Attracted 65+ volunteers who collected over 400 pounds of litter
- **Canada Day Celebration** — Attended by approximately 200 residents and families
- **Park Improvements** — Worked with the City of Edmonton on renewed pathway lighting and landscaping
- **Community Engagement** — Hosted four successful board meetings with strong resident attendance

### 2026 Budget Approval

Members approved the 2026 operating budget of $8,500, which will fund:
- Community events and celebrations
- Park maintenance and improvement initiatives
- Board meeting logistics and administration
- Neighbourhood communication and website maintenance

### Board Elections

The following residents were elected to serve on the RNA Board for 2026:

- **President:** Margaret Chen
- **Vice President:** David Thompson
- **Treasurer:** Sarah Williams
- **Secretary:** Michael Rodriguez
- **Member at Large:** Jennifer Martinez
- **Member at Large:** Robert Okafor

## Looking Ahead to 2026

The incoming board outlined their priorities for 2026:

- Expanding community engagement through additional neighbourhood social events
- Continuing partnership with the City on green space improvements
- Developing a Royalwood community directory for residents
- Hosting a neighbourhood skills-sharing workshop series

## Meeting Proceedings

The meeting followed Robert's Rules of Order and included:
- Approval of 2024 meeting minutes
- Treasurer's report and financial review
- President's report on accomplishments and challenges
- Open forum for resident questions and suggestions
- Elections

Light refreshments were served, and the meeting concluded at approximately 8:15 PM.

Thank you to all residents who attended and participated in this important community gathering!
