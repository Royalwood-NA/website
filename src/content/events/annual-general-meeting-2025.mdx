The Royalwood Neighbourhood Association Annual General Meeting (AGM) is the most important meeting of our year. It's your opportunity to hear directly from the board, ask questions, and vote on matters that affect our community.

## Agenda

1. Call to order and confirmation of quorum
2. Approval of previous AGM minutes
3. President's report
4. Treasurer's financial report
5. Proposed bylaw amendments (Sections 4 & 7) — discussion and vote
6. Election of board positions (two seats up for nomination)
7. Open floor — resident questions and comments
8. Adjournment

## Who Can Attend?

All Royalwood residents are welcome and encouraged to attend. Voting members are those who meet the criteria outlined in our bylaws. If you have questions about your eligibility, email [board@royalwoodna.org](mailto:board@royalwoodna.org).

## Refreshments

Light refreshments will be provided. Please let us know of any dietary restrictions when you RSVP.
