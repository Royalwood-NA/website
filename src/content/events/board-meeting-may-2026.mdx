You're invited to attend the Royalwood Neighbourhood Association Board Meeting for May 2026. All residents are welcome to attend and participate in discussions about issues affecting our neighbourhood.

## Event Details

**When:** Thursday, May 14, 2026 at 7:00 PM  
**Where:** Royalwood Community Centre, Meeting Room A  
**Address:** Royalwood Community Centre, Winnipeg, MB  
**Duration:** Typically 90 minutes  
**Admission:** Free and open to all residents

## May Agenda Preview

The May meeting will cover several important topics:

- **Park Maintenance Update** — Discussion of recent improvements and planned maintenance at Royalwood Park and other neighbourhood green spaces
- **2026 Budget Review** — Review and discussion of the RNA's operating budget for 2026
- **Community Event Planning** — Updates on upcoming neighbourhood events including the Canada Day celebration
- **Resident Concerns** — Open forum for residents to raise questions and concerns
- **Election Announcements** — Information about upcoming board elections

## How to Participate

All residents are welcome to attend! You don't need to be a member or register in advance. Simply come to the meeting room. We recommend arriving a few minutes early to find parking and get situated.

### Submitting Agenda Items

If you have a specific issue you'd like the board to address, please submit your item in advance by emailing us through our contact page. Please include a brief description of the topic and your name.

## What to Expect

Board meetings are conducted in a friendly, collaborative atmosphere. We cover business items efficiently but always make time for resident input and questions. Coffee and light refreshments are provided.

If you have any questions about the meeting or need special accommodations, please let us know through our contact page.

We look forward to seeing you there!
