Join us for a festive Canada Day celebration right here in Royalwood! This is our annual opportunity to celebrate our nation and strengthen the bonds within our neighbourhood community.

## Event Details

**When:** Wednesday, July 1, 2026 from 2:00 PM onwards  
**Where:** Royalwood Cul-de-sac on Sagebrush Way  
**What to bring:** Your family and friends! We'll provide the main BBQ items, but feel free to bring a dish to share.

## What's Happening

We're planning a full afternoon of celebration featuring:

- **Community BBQ** with hot dogs, hamburgers, and veggie options
- **Games and activities** for all ages, including lawn games and water activities
- **Live music** and entertainment throughout the afternoon
- **Fireworks viewing** as the sun sets (weather permitting)
- **Canada-themed crafts** for the kids

## Perfect for Families

This is a fantastic family-friendly event where residents of all ages can celebrate together. Whether you're new to the neighbourhood or longtime residents, Canada Day is a great time to connect with your neighbours and celebrate what makes our country special.

Bring a lawn chair or blanket to sit on, sunscreen, and your patriotic spirit! We'll have shaded areas available for those who prefer to stay out of the direct sun.

## Getting Involved

We welcome volunteers to help set up, run activities, and clean up after the event. If you'd like to contribute to making this Canada Day celebration special, please let us know through our contact page.

See you there as we celebrate together!
