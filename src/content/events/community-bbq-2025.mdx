The Royalwood NA summer BBQ is back! Come out and enjoy the long summer evening with your neighbours. This is one of our most popular events of the year and a great way to meet the people who live near you.

## What to Expect

- Free burgers, hot dogs, and veggie options (while supplies last)
- Lawn games — bocce, cornhole, horseshoes
- Face painting for kids (3:00–5:00 PM)
- A brief update from the board on neighbourhood initiatives

## Details

**Date:** Saturday, July 19, 2025  
**Time:** 3:00 PM – 8:00 PM  
**Rain date:** Sunday, July 20, 2025  

## Volunteering

We need 8–10 volunteers to help with setup, grilling, and cleanup. If you can spare a few hours, please [get in touch](/get-involved). Volunteers get first dibs on the good burgers.
