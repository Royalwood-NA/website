As the leaves start to turn and the season winds down, it's time to give Royalwood one final tidy before the snow arrives. Join us for our fall cleanup — a community tradition that makes a real, visible difference.

## Focus Areas This Year

Based on feedback from last year's participants, we'll be focusing on:

- **Storm pond perimeter** — litter removal and light vegetation clearing
- **Pathway edges** — removing debris and overgrowth before it gets buried under snow
- **Park benches and signage** — minor repairs and cleaning

## What We Provide

- Work gloves (various sizes)
- Biodegradable garbage bags
- Hot coffee and tea throughout the morning
- Snacks

## What to Bring

- Sturdy footwear (the ground may be wet)
- Weather-appropriate clothing
- Your enthusiasm!

Families with children are very welcome. We'll have age-appropriate tasks for kids 8 and up.
