The 2025 Halloween Parade & Costume Contest was a spooktacular success! Here's a look back at this year's festive celebration in the Royalwood neighbourhood.

## Event Recap

On October 31st, the Royalwood neighbourhood was filled with the sights and sounds of Halloween celebration. Over 120 costumed children and their families gathered at Royalwood Park for our annual Halloween event.

## Parade Highlights

The parade kicked off at 5:30 PM with a vibrant procession through the neighbourhood. Participants wore costumes ranging from classic spooks and superheroes to creative DIY creations. Several parents even joined their children in costume, making it a truly family-friendly celebration.

Local businesses supported the event by donating candy and treats for distribution along the parade route. Volunteers stationed throughout ensured everyone had a safe, fun experience.
