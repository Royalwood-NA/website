Mark your calendars for our annual Neighbourhood-Wide Garage Sale! This is the perfect opportunity to find great deals, meet your neighbours, and clear out items you no longer need.

## Event Details

**When:** Saturday, August 15, 2026 from 8:00 AM to 2:00 PM  
**Where:** Multiple locations throughout the Royalwood neighbourhood  
**Admission:** Free

## How It Works

Residents interested in participating can register their address to be included on the official garage sale map. Maps will be available online and printed copies will be distributed throughout the neighbourhood.

### Benefits of Participating

- **Reach more buyers** — Your address will be on the map distributed to hundreds of potential shoppers
- **Community exposure** — Help promote your garage sale across the whole neighbourhood
- **Easy coordination** — We'll handle map creation and distribution
- **Build connections** — Meet neighbours while shopping and selling

## For Shoppers

If you're looking to find great deals, keep an eye on our website and social media for the garage sale map as we get closer to the event. You'll be able to see all participating addresses and plan your route!

Arrive early for the best selection, but remember that many sellers start putting out items the evening before.

## Tips for Sellers

- **Price items fairly** — Garage sales are about finding items homes, not making maximum profit
- **Display clearly** — Arrange items so they're easy to browse
- **Have change ready** — Bring plenty of small bills and coins
- **Be friendly** — Garage sales are great opportunities to meet neighbours
- **Plan for the heat** — Provide yourself with shade and water on the August morning

## Registering Your Address

To register your address for inclusion on the garage sale map, please fill out the form on our contact page. Registration deadline is **August 5, 2026**.

Questions? Feel free to reach out through our contact page. We're excited to see the Royalwood neighbourhood come together for this annual event!
