It's that time of year again! The Royalwood Neighbourhood Association is excited to invite all residents to our annual Spring Community Cleanup. Whether you're looking to make a difference in your neighbourhood or just want to spend time with your neighbours, this is a fantastic opportunity to get involved.

## Event Details

**When:** Sunday, May 3, 2026 from 10:00 AM to 1:00 PM  
**Where:** Meet at Royalwood Park main entrance  
**What to bring:** A good attitude and the desire to help! We'll provide gloves, garbage bags, grabber tools, and light refreshments.

## What We'll Be Doing

Our spring cleanup focuses on clearing litter from streets, pathways, and green spaces throughout the Royalwood neighbourhood. Teams of volunteers will spread out across our community to tackle everything from picking up debris along major corridors to clearing pathways in our parks.

The City of Edmonton has been incredibly supportive of our efforts and will have a waste truck on-site to collect all the materials we gather. This makes the process smooth and ensures everything is disposed of responsibly.

## Why Participate?

Beyond the immediate impact of cleaning up our neighbourhood, participants will:

- **Meet and connect** with fellow residents and build community bonds
- **Contribute to a healthier community** with a cleaner, more attractive neighbourhood
- **Set a positive example** for younger residents about environmental stewardship
- **Enjoy complimentary refreshments** while working alongside your neighbours
- **Feel the satisfaction** of making a tangible difference in your community

## What to Expect

This is a family-friendly event. Children are welcome, and we'll have age-appropriate tasks available. The event is typically completed by 1:00 PM, leaving the rest of your Sunday free.

If you have any questions or would like to help organize the cleanup, please reach out to the RNA Board through our contact page. We look forward to seeing you there!
