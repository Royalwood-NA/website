In addition to the bylaws, the RNA board operates under a set of operational policies adopted to ensure consistent, fair, and transparent governance.

## Conflict of Interest

Board members must declare any conflict of interest — real or perceived — before discussion of a relevant agenda item. A member with a declared conflict may provide information but must recuse themselves from the vote.

## Financial Controls

- Expenditures under $500 may be approved by the Treasurer without a board vote
- Expenditures between $500 and $2,000 require email approval from a majority of board members
- Expenditures over $2,000 require a formal board vote at a scheduled meeting
- All expenditures are reported in the monthly treasurer's update

## Communication Policy

The board communicates with residents through:
- This website (primary)
- Email newsletter (subscribe on the [Get Involved](/get-involved) page)
- Notices posted on community bulletin boards at park entrances

Board members do not speak on behalf of the RNA in personal social media posts or neighbourhood Facebook groups. Official positions are communicated through official channels only.

## Records Retention

Meeting minutes, financial records, and official correspondence are retained for a minimum of seven years. Requests for historical records can be directed to [secretary@royalwoodna.org](mailto:secretary@royalwoodna.org).
