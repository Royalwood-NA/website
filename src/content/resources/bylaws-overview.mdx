The Royalwood Neighbourhood Association operates under a set of bylaws that define our purpose, membership structure, governance, and financial accountability. These bylaws were last formally updated in 2022, with proposed amendments to be voted on at the 2025 AGM.

## Purpose

The RNA exists to:

- Represent the interests of Royalwood residents with the City of Edmonton and other authorities
- Foster a sense of community through social, cultural, and environmental initiatives
- Maintain open communication between residents and the board
- Manage neighbourhood resources responsibly and transparently

## Membership

Any adult resident of Royalwood is eligible for membership. Voting rights are currently tied to property ownership, though proposed amendments would extend voting to all residents (see [2025 Bylaw Amendment Notice](/blog/bylaw-amendment-notice)).

## Board Structure

The board consists of seven elected positions:

| Position | Term |
|---|---|
| President | 2 years |
| Vice President | 2 years |
| Treasurer | 2 years |
| Secretary | 2 years |
| Three Directors-at-Large | 2 years (staggered) |

Elections are held at the AGM. Any eligible member may run for a board seat.

## Amendments

Bylaw amendments require a two-thirds majority vote at a general meeting, with at least 30 days' written notice to members prior to the vote.

For a copy of the full bylaws document, contact [board@royalwoodna.org](mailto:board@royalwoodna.org).
