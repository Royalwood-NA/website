### 1.2 City Website
**winnipeg.ca** – Access all City services, pay bills, submit requests, find information on every aspect of City services

### 1.3 In-Person Services
**City of Winnipeg Service Centres:**
- Multiple locations across Winnipeg
- Hours: Monday–Friday 8:30 AM – 4:30 PM
- Services: Permits, licenses, property tax questions, utility payments

## 2. REPORTING ISSUES

### 2.1 Potholes & Road Maintenance
**Report via:**
- Phone: 311
- Online: winnipeg.ca/311
- Mobile App: City of Winnipeg 311 app
- Timeline: Patching typically occurs within 7–30 days depending on severity

### 2.2 Graffiti Removal
**Report via:**
- Phone: 311
- Online: winnipeg.ca/311
- Description needed: Location, type of surface, size of graffiti
- Timeline: City typically responds within 5–10 business days

### 2.3 Damaged Sidewalks
**Report via:**
- Phone: 311
- Description: Location and nature of damage (crack, heave, broken concrete)
- Timeline: Assessment within 30 days; repairs vary based on severity

### 2.4 Street Lights
**Report burned-out or malfunctioning street lights:**
- Phone: 311
- Online: winnipeg.ca/311
- Include: Street name and nearest intersection

### 2.5 Parks & Green Spaces
**Report issues:**
- Phone: 311
- Issues: Broken equipment, damaged facilities, debris, maintenance concerns

## 6. PERMITS & APPROVALS

### 6.1 Building & Development Permits
**Contact:** City Planning & Development Department
**Phone:** 311
**Online:** winnipeg.ca/ppd
**Required for:** Home additions, renovations, fencing, decks, sheds, major exterior work

### 6.2 Typical Permit Types
- **Building Permit:** Structural changes, renovations
- **Development Permit:** Zoning compliance, building placement
- **Fence Permit:** Installation in some areas
- **Plumbing Permit:** Fixture installation or major repairs
- **Electrical Permit:** Electrical work (most jurisdictions)

### 6.3 Permit Process
1. Submit application with plans and documentation
2. City reviews for code compliance (typically 10–20 business days)
3. Approval or request for modifications
4. Work can begin once permit is issued
5. Inspections required at various stages (foundation, framing, final)

### 6.4 Zoning & Land Use
**For questions about:**
- What's allowed on your property
- Setback requirements
- Land use designations
**Contact:** City Planning, Property & Development, 311
