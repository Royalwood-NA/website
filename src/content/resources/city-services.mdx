Royalwood residents are served by the City of Edmonton for most municipal services. Here's a quick reference for the most commonly needed contacts and programs.

## 311 — Your First Stop

**311** is the City of Edmonton's main service line. Use it (or the 311 app) to:
- Report potholes, sidewalk damage, or pathway issues
- Report graffiti or vandalism on public property
- Request garbage, recycling, or yard waste pickup changes
- Report a missed collection
- Ask about any City service or program

**Hours:** Monday–Friday, 7 AM – 7 PM  
**After hours:** Limited service available  
**Online:** [311.edmonton.ca](https://311.edmonton.ca)  
**App:** Available on iOS and Android

## Waste & Recycling

The City provides three-stream waste collection in Royalwood:

| Cart colour | Contents |
|---|---|
| Black | Landfill / garbage |
| Blue | Recycling (paper, cardboard, plastics 1–7, glass, metal) |
| Green | Organics / food scraps and yard waste |

Collection day for Royalwood: **[Check the City's schedule tool](https://www.edmonton.ca/waste)**

**Large Item Pickup:** Available by appointment through 311 or the app.

**Eco Stations:** Drop off hazardous waste, electronics, and large recyclables at Eco Stations. The nearest one is at **5205 – 59 Avenue NW**. Hours vary seasonally.

## Road & Sidewalk Maintenance

- **Pothole reporting:** 311 app (include a photo and location)
- **Snow clearing priority:** Royalwood's residential streets are typically Category 3 (cleared within 48–72 hours of a major snowfall)
- **Sidewalk snow:** Property owners are responsible for clearing adjacent sidewalks within 48 hours of snowfall

## Utilities

**EPCOR** provides water, wastewater, and drainage services in Edmonton.  
**For billing and outages:** [epcor.com](https://www.epcor.com) or 1-800-668-5506

**ATCO** provides natural gas service.  
**For emergencies (gas leak):** 1-800-511-3447 (24 hours)

**ENMAX** provides electricity.  
**For outages:** [enmax.com](https://www.enmax.com) or 310-4300
