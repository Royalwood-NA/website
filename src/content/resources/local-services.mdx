Royalwood is a well-serviced residential neighbourhood in southeast Edmonton. Here's a roundup of what's nearby.

## Grocery & Retail

- **Millwoods Town Centre** — Major grocers, pharmacy, bank branches, and retail. Approximately 10 minutes by car or 20 minutes by bus.
- **Tamarack Common** — Newer commercial area with grocery, restaurant, and service options.

## Healthcare

- **Millwoods Health Centre** — Walk-in clinic, lab services, and specialty referrals. Call ahead to confirm wait times.
- **Pharmacy** — Multiple options at Millwoods Town Centre and along 66 Street.

## Libraries

**Millwoods Branch — Edmonton Public Library**  
Visit [epl.ca](https://www.epl.ca) for hours and programs.

## Places of Worship

Several churches, a gurdwara, and a mosque serve the broader Millwoods area. A complete list is available through the [City of Edmonton community directory](https://www.edmonton.ca).
