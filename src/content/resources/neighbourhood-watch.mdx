Royalwood has an active Neighbourhood Watch program coordinated in partnership with the Edmonton Police Service. The program is built on a simple principle: neighbours looking out for neighbours.

## How It Works

Neighbourhood Watch is not a vigilante program. Participants do not confront suspicious individuals or take enforcement action. Instead, participants:

1. **Observe** — Be aware of what's normal in your block
2. **Report** — Call EPS non-emergency (780-423-4567) or 911 if witnessing a crime in progress
3. **Connect** — Stay in touch with your block captain and neighbours

## Our Block Captains

Royalwood is divided into watch zones, each with a volunteer block captain who acts as a point of contact. To find out who your block captain is, email [watch@royalwoodna.org](mailto:watch@royalwoodna.org).

## Getting Involved

New participants are always welcome. You do not need to commit to a large time investment — even simply introducing yourself to your immediate neighbours and agreeing to keep an eye out is valuable.

To join or volunteer as a block captain:
- Email [watch@royalwoodna.org](mailto:watch@royalwoodna.org)
- Attend the next RNA general meeting and speak to a board member
- Visit the [Get Involved](/get-involved) page

## Reporting Tips

- **In progress crime or emergency:** Call **911**
- **Suspicious activity (non-emergency):** Call EPS at **780-423-4567**
- **Online reporting (minor incidents):** [eps.ca/online-reporting](https://www.edmontonpolice.ca/ReportaCrime)
- **Anonymous tips:** [Crime Stoppers 1-800-222-8477](https://www.crimestoppers.org)
